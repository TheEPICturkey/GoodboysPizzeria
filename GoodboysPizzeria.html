About the Founder:

Goodboy is the goodest boy, born approximately 11/27/2009, he created this heartfelt, family driven resteraunt. He loved tuna, chicken, kibble, greenies, anchovies, gravy, and birds. You would never find anything healthy on his dish as that wasnt his style. He was teased growing up for his weird tastes. from that moment he decided when he grew up he would create what is now known as "Goodboys Pizzeria", the acclaimed five star resteraunt, for those with the same tastes as him.
